About Me

I am a talented tattoo artist, photographer, and skilled technician in computer and camera repair, I combine my artistic vision with technical expertise to offer a range of creative and practical services. As a tattoo artist, I bring clients' ideas to life with precision and creativity, producing unique, custom designs that tell personal stories. My photography skills capture unforgettable moments, blending a keen eye for detail with a mastery of lighting and composition to deliver stunning visuals. On the technical side, I adept at diagnosing and repairing both computers, phone and cameras, ensuring that clients' essential tools remain in top condition. This blend of artistry and technical skill allows me to meet the diverse needs of their clientele, offering everything from personalized tattoos and professional photo shoots to expert device repairs.
